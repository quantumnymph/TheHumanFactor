The Human Factor

The human factor is an old-school style text adventure game series proposed as a concept by a graphic design student researching the connection between decision making and connotations in an environment where the choice is put into context. The premise is that there could be a difference in answers when the subject is asked about a connection indirectly rather than directly, thus exploring a subconscious connection. Basically, the goal of the games is to analyse the answers of players in accordance to expected connotations of certain answers as distributed by age groups, gender and education.
